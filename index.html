WELCOME to our Bistro/Scratch Kitchen!

Lake George's newest restaurant. Our Management & Chefs have over 20 years' experience in the restaurant industry and would love for you and your family to come and experience our fabulous cuisine. Try our signature Lobster Roll, Fresh Seafood, Pasta dishes, Signature Salads, Homemade soups, and more. We also offer take-out, reservations for parties, and special event catering. Our menu is family friendly with an American/ South Florida flare. We also have great view for fireworks viewing on Thursday evenings! (The fireworks show begins at 9:30pm)
Having a large gathering? Give us a call, we offer family style platters to go!
